I am glad to see that the news section of the website has been completed. Although there is a slight difference from expectations, it is basically usable. Now I am writing this article to explain how to publish news.


Due to the difficulty of identity verification, I plan to allow all team members to publish news by uploading a formatted markdown file to a specific folder in the GitHub repository. Here are the steps for publishing a news article:


------------


------------


------------



Firstly, you need to prepare a markdown editor that can be used to assist you in writing articles.

------------


**Secondly, when you start writing, add the following code to the beginning of your article.**


```
---
layout: post
title:  "NewsTitle - Authentic"
date:   2026-04-04 12:00:00
---

```


The 'title' is used to fill in the article title and author name. Since I was unable to independently showcase the author, I propose using 'Article Title - Author' to label the author.

The 'date' is the date, just fill in according to the requirements.

Please do not change the 'layout'.

------------


Thirdly, when you finish writing and exporting the markdown, change the file name to '''XXXX-XX-XX-ID.md''' (XXXX-XX-XX is the date, for example, 2026-01-01)

ID will not be displayed.

------------


------------
