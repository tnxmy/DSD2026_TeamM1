## Part III: Non-Functional Requirements

<span class="section-label">Quality Attributes</span>

<div class="info-block">
  These quality attributes apply to the entire M1 application and define the standards that must be met across all functional areas.
</div>

<hr class="section-divider">


## 6. Non-Functional Requirements


### 6.1 Performance and Latency

<div class="nfr-grid">
  <div class="nfr-card"><div class="nfr-id">NFR-M1-01</div><div class="nfr-text">Real-time visualisation must update within <strong>200ms</strong> of each incoming data frame to maintain perceived immediacy.</div></div>
  <div class="nfr-card"><div class="nfr-id">NFR-M1-02</div><div class="nfr-text">Posture alerts must be displayed within <strong>100ms</strong> of receiving an alert payload.</div></div>
  <div class="nfr-card"><div class="nfr-id">NFR-M1-03</div><div class="nfr-text">App launch to login screen must take less than <strong>3 seconds</strong> on a mid-range device.</div></div>
  <div class="nfr-card"><div class="nfr-id">NFR-M1-04</div><div class="nfr-text">Server requests must have a client-side timeout of <strong>10 seconds</strong> with a clear error message on expiry.</div></div>
  <div class="nfr-card"><div class="nfr-id">NFR-M1-05</div><div class="nfr-text">The 3D rendering pipeline must maintain at least <strong>30 frames per second</strong> on the target device class.</div></div>
</div>

<hr class="section-divider">


### 6.2 Usability

<div class="nfr-grid">
  <div class="nfr-card"><div class="nfr-id">NFR-M1-06</div><div class="nfr-text">The app must be usable by patients with <strong>no prior technical knowledge</strong>. No technical terminology shall be exposed in the patient-facing UI.</div></div>
  <div class="nfr-card"><div class="nfr-id">NFR-M1-07</div><div class="nfr-text">All critical actions (start session, end session, connect sensor) must be reachable within <strong>2 taps</strong> from the dashboard.</div></div>
  <div class="nfr-card"><div class="nfr-id">NFR-M1-08</div><div class="nfr-text">Posture correction messages must be written in plain language: e.g., <em>"Bend your knee more"</em> rather than <em>"Deviation: -24.8°"</em>.</div></div>
  <div class="nfr-card"><div class="nfr-id">NFR-M1-09</div><div class="nfr-text">Font sizes and contrast ratios must meet <strong>WCAG AA</strong> accessibility standards.</div></div>
  <div class="nfr-card"><div class="nfr-id">NFR-M1-10</div><div class="nfr-text">Error messages must always offer a next step. <strong>No dead-end error states</strong> permitted.</div></div>
</div>

<hr class="section-divider">


### 6.3 Security

<div class="nfr-grid">
  <div class="nfr-card"><div class="nfr-id">NFR-M1-11</div><div class="nfr-text">All network communication must use <strong>encrypted transport</strong>.</div></div>
  <div class="nfr-card"><div class="nfr-id">NFR-M1-12</div><div class="nfr-text">Session tokens must be stored using platform secure storage (<strong>iOS Keychain / Android Keystore</strong>).</div></div>
  <div class="nfr-card"><div class="nfr-id">NFR-M1-13</div><div class="nfr-text">No biometric or health data may be <strong>logged to device console or crash logs</strong>.</div></div>
  <div class="nfr-card"><div class="nfr-id">NFR-M1-14</div><div class="nfr-text">The app must comply with <strong>GDPR</strong> requirements for health data. Patients must consent before data collection begins.</div></div>
  <div class="nfr-card"><div class="nfr-id">NFR-M1-15</div><div class="nfr-text">Session tokens must be <strong>invalidated on logout</strong>.</div></div>
</div>

<hr class="section-divider">


### 6.4 Reliability

<div class="nfr-grid">
  <div class="nfr-card"><div class="nfr-id">NFR-M1-16</div><div class="nfr-text">Data stream disconnection during an active session must trigger <strong>automatic reconnection with exponential backoff</strong>. Patient notified if reconnection fails after 30 seconds.</div></div>
  <div class="nfr-card"><div class="nfr-id">NFR-M1-17</div><div class="nfr-text">BLE disconnection must not cause <strong>data loss</strong> for frames already received and processed.</div></div>
  <div class="nfr-card"><div class="nfr-id">NFR-M1-18</div><div class="nfr-text">The app must handle <strong>intermittent network conditions gracefully</strong> without crashing.</div></div>
</div>

<hr class="section-divider">


### 6.5 Maintainability

<div class="nfr-grid">
  <div class="nfr-card"><div class="nfr-id">NFR-M1-19</div><div class="nfr-text">The app must be built with <strong>React Native (Expo) + TypeScript</strong> following M1 architectural conventions.</div></div>
  <div class="nfr-card"><div class="nfr-id">NFR-M1-20</div><div class="nfr-text">All components must be tested by the <strong>cross-testing model</strong> defined in the team methodology.</div></div>
  <div class="nfr-card"><div class="nfr-id">NFR-M1-21</div><div class="nfr-text">All commit messages, PR descriptions, and code comments must be written in <strong>English</strong>.</div></div>
</div>

### 7.1 Assumptions

<div class="open-item"><div class="open-item-dot resolved"></div><p><strong>A-M1-05:</strong> The IMU sensors communicate via <strong>Bluetooth Low Energy</strong>. M1 does not handle WiFi-based sensor protocols.</p></div>
<div class="open-item"><div class="open-item-dot resolved"></div><p><strong>A-M1-06:</strong> Exercise plans are created exclusively in <strong>M2 by doctors</strong> and delivered to M1 via V2. M1 has no plan-creation functionality.</p></div>
<div class="open-item"><div class="open-item-dot resolved"></div><p><strong>A-M1-07:</strong> Push notification delivery is handled via an external service configured by V2. M1 only handles registration and display.</p></div>
<div class="open-item"><div class="open-item-dot resolved"></div><p><strong>A-M1-08:</strong> Target devices run <strong>iOS 14+</strong> or <strong>Android 9+</strong>.</p></div>
<div class="open-item"><div class="open-item-dot resolved"></div><p><strong>A-M1-09:</strong> IF2 interface contract will be frozen by the end of <strong>Sprint 2</strong>. M1 will use mock data following the agreed schema until the contract is confirmed.</p></div>
